Grandma and Grandpa's Mac-a-Weenie

Serves: a loving family

A better version of mac and cheese, made with love.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 mined onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 pkg hot dogs
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Cook and drain macaroni according to package instructions.
  3. In a small pan, melt 1 tbsp of butter over medium heat.
  4. Once butter is melted, add minced onion, salt and garlic powder. Cook until onions are transparent.
  5. Add hot dogs to pan, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a large boil, combine the cooked pasta, hot dog, onions, cottage cheese, sour cream and cheddar cheese. Mix until well combined.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a 2 quart cassarole and top with extra shredded cheddar cheese.
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 min.
  9. Enjoy with your favorite lactose-tolerant family members who don't slander cottage cheese or sour cream!
